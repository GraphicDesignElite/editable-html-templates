Corporate Maintenance Checklist*

  • Place Your Order With MyCorporation®
  • Receive Your Articles of Incorporation
  • Keep Articles of Incorporation and Incorporator’s Certificate in a safe place
    • The Articles of Incorporation are the basic charter of the Corporation.
    • The Incorporator’s Certificate transfers all rights and responsibilities in the Corporation from the Incorporator to the Directors of the Corporation.
  • Draft Bylaws
    • The Bylaws are the written agreement that outlines the Corporation’s operation and management. The Bylaws generally contain information regarding shareholders and directors meetings, stock interests, notices, duties of directors and officers, and other routine conduct.
  • Complete the Waiver of Notice, Notice, and Minutes of Shareholder and Director Meetings as held
    • The Waiver of Notice, Notice, and Minutes of Shareholder and Director Meetings of the Corporation are the written record of meetings and are typically completed following: (1) the first meeting of the Shareholders and Directors, (2) the annual meetings of the Shareholders and Directors, and (3) any interim meetings of the Shareholders and Directors to document decisions made during the meetings.
  • Issue Stock Certificates
    • A Stock Certificate is a legal document of the Corporation that signifies ownership in a certain percentage of the Corporation.
  • Obtain/Confirm Federal EIN & File S-Corp Election (if desired)
    • The Federal Employer Identification Number (EIN) is a 9-digit number assigned to the Corporation to establish a business tax account, and is generally required for banking and tax purposes.
    • The S-Corp Election (Form 2553) is the form required to be filed with the IRS in order to elect to be taxed as an S-Corporation.
  • Contact MyCorporation
    • If you would like assistance with any of the above corporate compliance forms, contact MyCorporation.
      • Bylaws, Waivers of Notice and Stock Certificates are available for purchase for your internal corporate records.
      • o MyCorporation can also obtain your Federal EIN or prepare S-Corp election forms.
